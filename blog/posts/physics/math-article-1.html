Mathematics in the Digital Age

Posted on September 28, 2025

Mathematics and computing have always been intertwined. Here are some fundamental equations:

Euler's Identity:

\[e^{i\pi} + 1 = 0\]

The Fourier Transform:

\[\hat{f}(\xi) = \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} f(x) e^{-2\pi i x \xi} dx\]

These elegant formulas demonstrate the beauty of mathematical expression.
