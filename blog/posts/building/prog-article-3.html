Vibecoding my nintendo style portfolio page

Posted on October 3, 2025

When I first saw the yamauchi no10 website (it was featured in a discord post) I instantly had a stroke of inspiration. I wanted to create a portfolio page that was my portfolio page in retro nintendo style. I have never done any webdev in my life -- and I'm not planning on learning it either, so I was stuck at the idea phase.

The site

I was attending a coding workshop, and the presenter used windsurf for a complex ml task -- rather effectively. Inspired by that, I decided to try vibecoding my ideas. In the mean time, I found a cool css template: [NES.css](https://nostalgic-css.github.io/NES.css/#).
